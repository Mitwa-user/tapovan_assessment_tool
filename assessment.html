Tapovan Progressive Assessment Tool

Assessment

Scoring Key

  • 0: Dependent
  • 1: Occasionally
  • 2: Independent

Child Activities

  1. Can the child hold objects?
  2. Can the child crawl independently?
  3. Can the child walk without support?
  4. Can the child sit without support?
  5. Can the child climb stairs with support?
  6. Can the child recognize colors?
  7. Can the child recognize shapes?
  8. Can the child understand simple instructions?
  9. Can the child follow simple commands?
  10. Can the child use a spoon to feed himself/herself?
  11. Can the child play with toys?
  12. Can the child throw a ball?
  13. Can the child catch a ball?
