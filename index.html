Tapovan Progressive Assessment Tool

Instructions

  1. If the Child score 0-20%, they will be classified under the profound category.
  2. If the Child score 20-40%, they will be classified under the severe category.
  3. If the Child score 40-60%, they will be classified under the moderate category.
  4. If the Child score 60-80%, they will be classified under the mild category.
  5. If the Child score 80-100%, they will be classified under the normal category.
  6. If the child is 4 years old, choose activities for 4 years (1 to 10).
  7. If the child is 5 years old, choose activities for 4 and 5 years (1 to 20).
  8. If the child is 6 years old, choose activities for 4, 5, and 6 years (1 to 30).
  9. If the child is 7 years old, choose activities for 4, 5, 6, and 7 years (1 to 40).
  10. If the child is 8 years old, choose activities for 4, 5, 6, 7, and 8 years (1 to 50).
